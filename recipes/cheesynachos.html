Simple Nacho Recipe

This is a very simple recipe of few ingredients and a lot of flavor. It consists of Ground Beef or Turkey, cheese, tomoatoes, tomato paste, onions and cilantro. There are seasonings as well as measurements but that would double my ingredient list.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef or Turkey
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Onions
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Cook Ground Beef/Turkey
  2. Cook onions
  3. Add tomato paste
  4. Combine ingredients
  5. Add cheese
