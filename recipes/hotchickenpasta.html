Hot Chicken Pasta

A simple chicken pasta

This is a very simple recipe of few ingredients and a lot of flavor. It consists of chicken, cheese, rotini noodles, and greek dressing. There are seasonings as well as measurements but that would double my ingredient list.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
  • Tri-Color Rotini Noodles
  • Shredded Colby Jack
  • Greek Dressing

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken
  2. Season chicken with Old Bay & Garlic Salt
  3. Cook chicken
  4. Boil noodles
  5. Preheat oven to 350
  6. Combine chicken and noodles and add cheese
  7. Bake until cheese is melted
