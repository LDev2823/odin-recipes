Simple Chicken Quesadilla Recipe

This is a very simple recipe of few ingredients and a lot of flavor. It consists of chicken, cheese, tortillas, lettuce, and something to dip it all in. There are seasonings as well as measurements but that would double my ingredient list.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Tortillas
  • Lettuce
  • Sour Cream
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cut Lettuce
  2. Cook Chicken
  3. Grease pan with Olive Oil
  4. Toast tortillas
  5. Add chicken and cheese to tortillas
  6. Melt cheese
