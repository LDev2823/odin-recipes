Chicken Quesadillas

Simple Chicken Quesadilla recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Tortillas
  • Lettuce
  • Sour Cream
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cut Lettuce
  2. Cook Chicken
  3. Grease pan with Olive Oil
  4. Toast tortillas
  5. Add chicken and cheese to tortillas
  6. Melt cheese
